About Our Church

Friends Of Christ Assembly (FOCA) is a small, welcoming church dedicated to serving God and our community. We believe in the power of fellowship, prayer, and studying God's Word together.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples of Jesus Christ in our local community and beyond.
